Class: DamageSource

src/damagesource.DamageSource(nDice, dieType, modifier, damageType, isMagical)

Class representing a single source of damage, such as from a weapon or spell. The damage type and whether it's magical can be specified. Examples might be a damaging spell (firebolt), or a nonmagical or +1 weapon. A single damageSource should represent a single kind of die roll and damage type. Attacks can combine multiple damageSources as needed.
